The project

Sound, spirit,
and analog warmth.

Analog Prophet began as a personal exploration — a space away from commercial work where Toby could follow sounds purely for their own sake. Using an extensive collection of analog synthesisers and modular equipment, the project blends electronic textures with ethnic and world influences to create music that moves slowly, breathes deeply.

The music is often described as spiritual and meditative — fitting for the imagery the name evokes. It exists in the tradition of artists like Brian Eno, Harold Budd, and Klaus Schulze, while carrying its own quietly distinctive voice.

New material continues to emerge. The 2025 album Visions represents the latest chapter in an ongoing journey through sound.
