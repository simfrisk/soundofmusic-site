About

Crafted audio,
built on trust.

Torbjörn "Toby" Frisk is a Stockholm-based music producer, composer and sound designer who has spent over three decades at the intersection of music, technology, and storytelling.

With a deep love for synthesisers and analog gear, he brings a distinctive sonic character to every project, from 450-episode Swedish TV dramas to award-winning short films. His philosophy: "A good work is about good relations with people."

He works with publishers, broadcasters, filmmakers, and independent creators who value quality, care, and a producer who genuinely listens.

Selected Work

Recognized craft.

Best Sound Design, Rome International Movie Awards, July 2024

Sound design for Death to Platitudes, a Norwegian short comedy by Øyvind Olav Sydow Kleiveland, earned Toby Best Sound Design at the Rome International Movie Awards in July 2024.

The film, starring Helge Jordal as a priest, also picked up nominations across the New York Independent Film Awards and the Christian Film Festival, a strong multi-festival run for a tightly crafted short.

Other recent highlights: a Dolby Atmos re-recording of a 40-year retrospective concert, the ongoing Ankhoven children's series, and church streaming productions across Sweden.
