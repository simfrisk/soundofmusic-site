About

Crafted audio,
built on trust.

Torbjörn "Toby" Frisk is a Stockholm-based music producer, composer and sound designer who has spent over three decades at the intersection of music, technology, and storytelling.

With a deep love for synthesisers and analog gear, Toby brings a distinctive sonic character to every project — from intimate audiobooks to award-winning short films. His philosophy is simple: "A good work is about good relations with people."

He works with publishers, filmmakers, broadcasters, and independent creators who value quality, care, and a producer who genuinely listens.

Selected Work

Recognized craft.

Award winner

Sound design for Death to Platitudes, a Norwegian short film by Øyvind Olav Sydow Kleiveland, earned Toby the Best Sound Design award at the Rome International Movie Awards in 2024.

The film also picked up multiple nominations at the Christian Film Festival and was selected for the New York Independent Film Awards — a multi-award run for a tightly crafted short.

Other recent credits include a Dolby Atmos re-recording of a 40-year retrospective concert, children's programme audio for Ankhoven and Ottar Bäver, and ongoing church streaming productions across Sweden.

Music Project

Analog Prophet

Open on Spotify

Analog Prophet is Toby's personal music project — electronic ambient music with ethnic influences, designed for reflection and calm.

"The best beat of any music is the beat of your own heart."

The project draws from Toby's love of synthesisers and analog gear, weaving together textures and rhythms that feel both ancient and futuristic. Albums include Visions (2025) and Hana Ka'nima (2011).
